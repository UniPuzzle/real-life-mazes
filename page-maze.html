Labirinto della Masone – 2020

Designed by Franco Maria Ricci, Labirinto della Masone is a complex covering around eight hectares. The avenues of the labyrinth have been created using a particular species of bamboo called Phyllostachys Bissetii, but different kinds of bamboo species can be found in the small squares, where visitors can acquaint themselves better with the varieties of this surprising plant. Labirinto della Masone

Working Hours

Open every day from 10:30am to 7:00pm, including holidays. Closed on Tuesdays and on the public holidays of 25 December and 1 January. The ticket office is open until 6:00pm. Advised to allow at least one and a half hours to see the bamboo labyrinth, the galleries and the temporary exhibitions. Tickets to the labyrinth give unlimited entry and exit on the day of validity. Completely free from any architectural barriers, and may be visited by people of any age. Pets are allowed.

Admission

Reduced Price One-Day Children’s Ticket, € 10 For all visitors aged 6 to 12. Ordinary One-Day Ticket, € 18 For all visitors aged over 12. Free Entry One-Day Ticket for Children For all visitors aged under 6. School ticket, € 10 For all school trips organised as part of the educational programs. Group Ticket, € 15 For organised groups of more than 15 people who have arranged the visit with the booking department. All tickets are one-day passes, and allow unlimited entry and exit on the day of validity during opening times.
